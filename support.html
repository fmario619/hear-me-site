Frequently Asked Questions

How does Hear Me work?

Hear Me guides you and your partner through a structured listening exercise. One person speaks while the other listens and reflects back what they heard, then you switch roles.

Is my data private?

Yes. Hear Me stores only the names you enter, and that data never leaves your device. We have no servers and collect no analytics or personal information.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by the Apple App Store. You can request a refund through Apple's Report a Problem page.

Is this a therapy app?

No. Hear Me is a communication tool inspired by research-backed listening principles. It is not a substitute for professional therapy or counseling.

How do I delete my data?

Since all data is stored locally on your device, deleting the app removes all stored data permanently.
