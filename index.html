Hear Me™

A space for listening together

A guided listening exercise for couples, inspired by the Gottman Method.

Available on the App Store and Google Play

How it works

1

Check in

Each person selects how they're feeling before the conversation begins.

2

Speak

One person shares what's on their mind while the other listens without interrupting.

3

Reflect

The listener mirrors, validates, and empathizes with what they heard.

4

Check in again

The listener shares how they're feeling now. Then choose to switch roles or wrap up.

5

See your journey

At the end, see how both of your emotions shifted from start to finish.

Track your emotional journey

Hear Me™ tracks how you both feel at every stage of the conversation. See how your emotions shift from the moment you start to the moment you finish. Over time, these patterns help you understand each other more deeply.
