Resources

The research and reading behind Hear Me.

Research

  • The Power of Listening in Couples
    Kuhn et al., Journal of Family Psychology, 2018. Explores how listening during stress conversations affects relationship outcomes.
  • Listening and Perceived Partner Responsiveness
    Reis & Itzchakov, Current Opinion in Psychology, 2023. Looks at how quality listening helps partners feel understood and validated.
  • Effectiveness of Gottman Couple Therapy
    Davoodvandi et al., Iranian Journal of Psychiatry, 2018. A controlled study showing Gottman therapy improved marital adjustment and intimacy.
  • Effects of Briefly Interrupting Marital Conflict
    Gottman & Tabares, Journal of Marital and Family Therapy, 2018. Structured pauses during conflict and their effect on relationship satisfaction.
  • Gottman Seven Principles: In-Person vs. Online
    Zahl-Olsen et al., Journal of Marital and Family Therapy, 2024. Shows digital delivery of the Gottman program works as well as in-person.
